Why Nick Hanauer

A credible business voice making the case for economic fairness.

Nick Hanauer has spent decades building companies, investing in category-defining businesses, and helping shape the civic life of Seattle and beyond. He brings unusual authority to public debates about capitalism because he argues from inside the system, not from outside it.

His work connects business success, democratic resilience, and shared prosperity. The through-line is simple: societies thrive when growth reaches more people, more communities, and more workers.

Core Ideas

The arguments that define the work.

Markets need customers

Durable growth depends on broad purchasing power, not just wealth concentration at the top.

Democracy and economy are linked

Strong institutions, fair wages, and civic trust are not side issues. They are prerequisites for long-term prosperity.

Policy shapes possibility

Rules determine outcomes. Better policy can make innovation, competition, and shared success reinforce one another.
