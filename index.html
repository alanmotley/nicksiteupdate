About

A business leader with a clear public voice.

Nick Hanauer has spent decades founding companies, investing in transformative businesses, and contributing to public life in Seattle and beyond. His perspective is rooted in direct experience building inside the American economy.

His work focuses on the conditions that make economies stronger, communities healthier, and democratic institutions more resilient. Across business and civic life, the central question is how growth can be broader, sturdier, and more widely shared.

Ideas

Three themes at the center of the work.

Growth depends on participation

Strong economies are built when more people can contribute to and benefit from growth.

Democracy and prosperity reinforce one another

Healthy institutions, civic trust, and broad opportunity are part of the foundation for long-term economic success.

Policy helps shape outcomes

Public rules influence how markets behave, who gets included, and what kind of future economic growth creates.
